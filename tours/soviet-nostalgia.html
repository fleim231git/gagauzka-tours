Transnistria · PMR

Soviet Nostalgia Tour

⏱ 8–10 hours 👤 Guide: Masha 🚗 Private transport included

About This Tour

Cross the border into Transnistria — a breakaway state where the Soviet Union never ended. Walk streets lined with Lenin statues, explore a gallery of Soviet art and ideology, and eat in a USSR-themed restaurant where time stopped in 1991.

This is one of the most unique experiences in Eastern Europe — a living museum of Soviet life, complete with its own currency, flag, and military checkpoints. Masha will guide you through the history, the politics, and the everyday reality of life in this extraordinary place.

Main Stops

  • Petru Costin Gallery

    Private gallery of Soviet ideology — sculptures, mosaics, and artifacts reflecting USSR aesthetics.

  • Open Air Museum of Soviet Socialism

    Large-scale Soviet sculptures, monuments, and installations in the open air.

  • Bender Fortress

    Historic 16th-century fortress on the Dniester River — Ottoman and Eastern European heritage.

  • Tiraspol Walking Tour

    Main Square, government buildings, Soviet monuments, and central urban landmarks.

  • Chobruchi Village

    Preserved Soviet-era village with House of Culture, Lenin monument, and Rodin Park.

  • Soviet Themed Lunch

    Back to USSR Restaurant or authentic Soviet Stolovka — full nostalgic dining experience.
