Odessa · Black Sea

Odessa Seafood Tour

⏱ 10–12 hours 👤 Guide: Cristi 🚗 Private transport included

About This Tour

A full day in Odessa — one of the Black Sea's most legendary cities. Walk through the historical center with its eclectic architecture, descend the iconic Potemkin Stairs, explore the vibrant Privoz Market, and end the day with the freshest seafood on the waterfront.

Cristi knows Odessa like the back of his hand. He'll take you beyond the tourist trail — into the real courtyards, hidden streets, and local food spots that make this city unforgettable.

Main Stops

  • Historical City Center

    Walking tour with architecture, landmarks, and the authentic old Odessa atmosphere.

  • Potemkin Stairs & Primorsky Blvd

    Iconic staircase with panoramic sea views and scenic boulevard walk.

  • Deribasivska Street

    Main pedestrian street with cafes, street life, and vibrant city atmosphere.

  • Privoz Market

    Authentic local market experience with food, culture, and daily life.

  • Moldavanka District

    Old residential district with authentic courtyards and Odessa stories.

  • Fresh Seafood Lunch

    Black Sea fish, oysters, and traditional Odessa-style seafood at Chornomorka.
